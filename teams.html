SK Telecom T1

SKT has won the World Championship a total of 3 times alongside 2 MSI wins.

They have won their regional title 8 times. These titles also makes this team one of the most feared to play against internationally.

T1's Current Roster.

Gen.G

Gen.G have been to one Worlds event where they placed 13-16th.

They haven't won any regional playoffs.

Gen.G's Current Roster.

DragonX

DragonX haven't gone to any International events.

they haven't won any of their regional playoffs either.

DragonX's Current Roster

G2 Esports

G2 Have gone to 4 World events, most notably 2018 and 2019 where they placed 3rd/4th in 2018 and 2nd in 2019.

G2 went to MSI 2019 and won the event.
Other than that they have a total of 7 regional titles. They have won almost every season since they joined the EU LCS(LEC before it got rebranded).

G2 Spring 2020 Roster, Note that Perkz & Caps are going to swap roles in Summer 2020.

Fnatic

Fnatic have went to 7 World events and won the very first event in season 1.
Fnatic went to two MSI events, but without any success.

Fnatic was one of the first teams to play in the EU LCS which has also lead to their 7 regional titles.

Fnatic's Current Roster.

MAD Lions

MAD Lions is a fairly new team, but branded off the old Splyce, which went to 2019 Worlds, and got to top 8.

Neither MAD Lions or Splyce have won any regional titles yet, however in the spring 2020 season MAD, looked like a very strong contender.

MAD Lions Current Roster. Humanoid is the only member left from the old Splyce.

Cloud 9

Cloud 9 have gone to 6 world tournaments with their best result in 2018 where they placed in top 4.

In regional play they have got a total of 3 titles.

Cloud 9's Current Roster.

FlyQuest

FlyQuest have not gone to any international event nor won any regional titles yet.

FlyQuest's Current Roster.

Evil Geniuses

Evil Geniuses have not gone to any international event nor won any regional titles yet.
