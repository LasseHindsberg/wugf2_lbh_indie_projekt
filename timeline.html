Timeline over League of Legends Esports

  • Season One Worlds event - The first event

    With a prize pool of only 100,000 US Dollars, the first event was held at Dreamhack in Jönköping, Sweden.

  • Season Two - The start of Regional Playoffs

    The first kinds of regional playoffs begin, as a qualifying league for the world tournament at the end of the season.

  • World Championship Season Two - Season of the Network issues

    During the World Championship playoffs Riot Games experienced Network Issues and later revealed their new LAN Client which would be used for the later tournaments.

  • Pro Players Recognized as Professional Athletes

    Riot Games announced that the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services recognized League of Legends pro-players as professional athletes.

  • Mid Season Invitational Starts Up

    In 2015 Riot Games announces a second world tournament known as Mid Season Invitational set between the spring and summer playoffs.

  • Riot Games gets sponsor from Louis Vuitton™

    The 2019 World Playoffs gets designed a special Louis Vuitton™ designed trophy case along with prestige edition skin designs following their in game True Damage Skin Line.

    Louis Vuitton™ also released League Of Legends inspired designs for their clothes in their shops.
